How to Vote — Step by Step

Your complete beginner-friendly walkthrough.

1

Check Eligibility

Confirm you meet the age, citizenship, and residency requirements for your jurisdiction.

2

Register to Vote

Visit your local election office or use the online portal. Have your ID and proof of address ready.

3

Find Your Polling Station

Use your government's polling station locator tool to find your designated voting place.

4

Prepare Your Documents

Gather your voter ID, registration confirmation, and any other required identification.

5

Cast Your Vote

Visit your polling station on Election Day. Follow the instructions, mark your ballot, and submit it.

6

Verify & Track

Some jurisdictions let you track your ballot online to confirm it was received and counted.
